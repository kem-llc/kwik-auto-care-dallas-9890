Oil Changes & Routine Maintenance

Keep your engine healthy with regular oil changes and fluid checks.

Overview

Routine maintenance is the key to a long-lasting vehicle. We offer oil changes using quality synthetic and conventional oils, plus multi-point inspections to catch issues early. Stay on schedule with Kwik Auto Care.

What's included

  • Quick 30-minute oil change service
  • Complimentary fluid top-off and tire pressure check
  • Maintenance reminders to keep you on track
  • Extends engine life and improves fuel economy

How it works

Common questions

How often should I get an oil change?

Every 5,000–7,500 miles for most modern vehicles. Check your owner’s manual.

What type of oil do you use?

We offer conventional, synthetic blend, and full synthetic – we’ll recommend the best for your car.
