Overview

Nothing ruins a Dallas drive like a broken AC. We service all AC systems – from refrigerant recharge to compressor replacement. Our technicians ensure your system blows cold air efficiently.

What's included

  • Complete AC inspection and performance test
  • Eco-friendly refrigerant handling
  • Fast service – most AC repairs same day
  • Extended system life with proper maintenance

How it works

1

System Check

We inspect components: compressor, condenser, evaporator, and lines.

2

Diagnose Leaks

We use UV dye and electronic detectors to find leaks.

3

Repair & Recharge

We fix leaks, replace parts, and recharge refrigerant to manufacturer specs.

Common questions

How often should I recharge my AC?

Only if it's not cooling properly. A well-maintained system shouldn't need frequent recharges.

Can you fix any AC problem?

Yes, from minor leaks to full compressor failure. We’ll advise the best solution.
